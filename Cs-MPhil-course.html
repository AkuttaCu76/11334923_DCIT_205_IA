An Overview on Our MPhil Program

An MPhil (Master of Philosophy) program in Computer Science is an advanced postgraduate research degree. It emphasizes independent research and often serves as a precursor to a Ph.D. It is important for developing research skills and contributing to the academic understanding of computer science topics. We the people of the department of Computer Science will aid anyone who want to persue a MPhil degree in Computer Science as it is in our ability to do so with intensive research skills development and academics understanding of Computer Science related topics. The  program is structured into two semesters where each semester comes with its courses to take.
