Undergraduate Degree Courses

When one chooses to offer Computer Science and makes his/her way to the department, the department gives two choices to the Undergraduate degree courses namely; Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor(3:2:2:1) and as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. But one will have to excel in the following DCIT and Maths courses offered at Level 100 as a Mathematical Science student. Depending on one's grade in Level 100 will he/she be made to offer a Single Major in Computer Science or Major Minor in Computer Science and Statistics respectivly. The courses include; 

  • DCIT 102: COMPUTER HARDWARE FUNDAMENTALS AND CIRCUITS
  • MATH122: CALCULUS I
  • STAT112: INTRODUCTION TO STATISTICS AND PROBABILITY II
  • DCIT104: PROGRAMMING FUNDAMENTALS
  • MATH 126: ALGEBRA AND GEOMETRY

Also a pass in the following course will enable a boost in obtaining Computer Science as a Major.
UGRC 110: Academic Writing or UGRC 150: Critical Thinking and Practical Reasoning.
Also, every student in the Department of Computer Science must own his or her laptop with minimum specification of:
i3 64-bit Processor
8GB RAM
500GB HD or 256 SSD
