Greetings to a whole New World of Computeer Science

We are currently in the era of Information Technology, and at its essence is the discipline of Computer Science. It is imperative to emphasize the significance of studying computer science for society to fully leverage the advantages presented by Information Technology, leading to swift economic progress. Computer science encompasses the examination and creation of applications facilitating our daily interactions with computers, the corresponding software systems, and the methodologies employed to produce applications and systems that operate reliably, accurately, and ethically. The fundamental skill at the core of Computer Science is computer programming. Employers seeking Computer Scientists anticipate individuals proficient in programming, equipped with knowledge of contemporary techniques and tools, and possessing the ability to apply sound engineering principles to the development, construction, and maintenance of computer programs.

Our department provides a diverse range of courses, coupled with skill development, enabling students to pursue employment across various economic sectors. These courses not only prepare graduates for success in the computing industry but also cultivate their ability to contribute specialized skills within project or research teams. The curriculum serves as a foundation for further academic or research pursuits, offering exposure to both established and emerging technologies and active fields of research in Computer Science. This approach ensures graduates are well-prepared for immediate professional challenges and positioned for ongoing success in the dynamic field of Computer Science.

The Department offers two undergraduate degree programmes, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.

Our Organization

The University of Ghana stands as one of the foremost public universities in Africa, exerting a profound influence on individuals, the local region, and the global community. Whether propelling young minds into an expansive future or tackling significant contemporary challenges through fearless research endeavors, our impact is substantial. Annually educating over 38,000 students, including approximately 2.64% in the field of computer science, we are committed to translating ideas into tangible impact and effecting transformative change in the world.
